INTRODUTION

Al-Noor Academy was established in jan 1980 by famous professional teacher Muhammad Usama Taufeeq,
Today, we are proud to say that our physical presence extends throughout Pakistan with a vast network of 50 branches operating in major cities of Pakistan including Karachi, Lahore, Islamabad etc. Our branches are operating under the guidance of a team of over 500 teachers who educate almost 1000s of students on a daily basis.

Our Mission

Our mission is to educate every single person of Pakistan who really are the future of Pakistan and make our country stong economically, technically and educationally
